# Task 3 – AI SEO Blog & Content Cluster Generator

## Business Chosen
MIDNIGHT LOVE Café  
Location: Hyderabad

MIDNIGHT LOVE is a late-night café designed for students, couples, friends, and night owls who want a cozy place to relax and enjoy chai and snacks.

---

## Objective
The goal of this task was to design a prompt system that generates SEO-optimized blog content and content clusters for business websites.

---

## SEO Strategy

Primary Keyword:
Best late-night café in Hyderabad

Content Structure:
- 1 Pillar blog targeting the main keyword
- Supporting blogs targeting related topics
- Local SEO focus with Hyderabad references

---

## Tool Used
ChatGPT

---

## Outcome
Using the prompt system, an SEO content cluster was created including a pillar blog and supporting blogs designed to improve website visibility on search engines.
